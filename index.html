Explanation



The principle behind this setup is to ensure comprehensive surveillance of the GOP fence post using multiple cameras placed at varying distances and angles. The origin camera focuses directly on the post where the disturbance is detected, while the middle-range and near-range cameras provide broader and closer perspectives, respectively, ensuring full coverage. Additional cameras positioned further away offer a wider context and cross-verification by monitoring adjacent areas. The cameras are synchronized to provide real-time footage to a control tower, allowing operators to pinpoint and analyze disturbances accurately. This multi-layered approach enhances both detection and response capabilities in real-time.
